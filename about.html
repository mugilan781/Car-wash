Our Origin

Born From a Passion for Perfection

GlossForge Detail was founded in 2009 by Marcus Tyler, a car enthusiast who was frustrated by the lack of quality detailing options in Detroit. Starting with a single pressure washer and a set of hand polishers, Marcus worked out of a small garage, building a reputation one car at a time.

Word spread quickly. By 2013, GlossForge moved to its first dedicated facility. By 2018, we expanded to our current 8,000 sq ft state-of-the-art studio with 8 full-service bays and a team of 25 certified technicians.

Today, we're Detroit's most trusted name in premium auto detailing — but our obsession with quality has never changed. Every car that leaves our studio is a reflection of our commitment to excellence.

Our Journey

15 Years of Milestones

2009
The Beginning
Marcus Tyler starts GlossForge from a single-bay garage in Detroit with nothing but a pressure washer and a passion for cars.
2011
First 500 Clients
Word-of-mouth growth reaches 500 happy clients. Hired first 3 technicians and expanded to weekends.
2013
First Dedicated Studio
Moved to a 2,000 sq ft studio with 4 service bays. Launched ceramic coating packages.
2015
IDA Certification
Entire team became IDA certified. Won Detroit's "Best Auto Detailing" award for the first time.
2018
Major Expansion
Moved to current 8,000 sq ft flagship studio. Added mobile detailing fleet (6 vans).
2021
10,000 Cars Milestone
Celebrated detailing our 10,000th vehicle — a 1967 Shelby GT500. Launched online booking system.
2024
National Recognition
Featured in Auto Detailing World magazine. Won Best Detailing Studio — Midwest for 3rd consecutive year.
