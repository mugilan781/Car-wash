Booking Support

Booking FAQs

Frequently asked questions about appointment booking, changes, and studios.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?+
Yes, you can cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before your slot via the email link or by contacting our team.
How long does a detailing session take?+
Express washes take 30-45 minutes. Complete details take 3-5 hours. Ceramic coatings require your vehicle for 24-48 hours to cure.
What if there is bad weather on my booking date?+
If you booked studio detailing, weather doesn't affect us! For mobile detailing, we will contact you to reschedule or shift inside.
