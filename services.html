FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a full detail take?+
A full detail typically takes 4–8 hours depending on the vehicle size and condition. For ceramic coating, plan for 1–2 days as the coating needs cure time. We'll give you a precise estimate when you drop off your vehicle.
How often should I get my car detailed?+
For exterior maintenance, we recommend a wash every 2–4 weeks. A full interior/exterior detail every 3–6 months keeps your car in top condition. With ceramic coating, annual maintenance washes are sufficient.
Do you offer mobile detailing?+
Yes! Our mobile detailing fleet comes to your home or office. Mobile service is available for most packages except ceramic coating, which requires our controlled studio environment for proper application.
What products do you use?+
We use only professional-grade, industry-leading products including Gtechniq ceramic coatings, Meguiar's polishes, Carpro decontamination sprays, and Koch Chemie cleaners. All products are safe for your vehicle and the environment.
Is ceramic coating worth the investment?+
Absolutely. While the upfront cost is higher, ceramic coating lasts 5 years compared to wax at 3–6 months. It protects your paint from UV damage, chemical stains, and oxidation — preserving your car's resale value significantly. Many clients see it as a cost-saving investment over time.
What is your satisfaction guarantee?+
If you're not completely satisfied with your service, notify us within 24 hours and we'll redo any unsatisfactory work at absolutely no charge. We stand behind every service we perform.
