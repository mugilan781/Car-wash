Section 01
Overview

GlossForge Detail, LLC ("GlossForge," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website www.GlossForge.com and provides premium car wash and auto detailing services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Our Commitment: We take your privacy seriously. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes — ever.

Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of our website. This policy applies to all information collected through our website, mobile application (when available), online booking forms, and any related services.

Section 02
Data We Collect

We collect information in the following categories, depending on how you interact with us:

Personal Information You Provide

  • Identity Data: First name, last name, username or similar identifier.
  • Contact Data: Email address, telephone number, and billing/service address.
  • Transaction Data: Details about payments and services you've purchased from us.
  • Booking Data: Appointment dates, vehicle details (year, make, model, color), and service preferences.
  • Account Data: Username and password when you create an account on our platform.
  • Communication Data: Messages, feedback, or survey responses you send us.

We may receive information about you from third parties, such as social media platforms (if you connect your account), payment processors, or analytics providers. We only use such data as permitted by this policy and applicable law.

Section 03
How We Use Your Data

We use your data only for legitimate business purposes. Below are the specific ways we use the information we collect:

  1. Service Delivery: Processing and confirming your appointments, delivering the requested detailing services, and communicating about your bookings.
  2. Account Management: Creating and maintaining your customer account, including managing your login credentials and service history.
  3. Payment Processing: Processing payments securely via our PCI-compliant third-party payment partners.
  4. Customer Support: Responding to your inquiries, complaints, and requests for assistance.
  5. Service Improvements: Analyzing usage patterns to improve our website, services, and overall customer experience.
  6. Marketing Communications: Sending promotional emails, offers, and newsletters — only if you have opted in. You may opt out at any time.
  7. Legal Compliance: Complying with applicable laws, regulations, court orders, and lawful requests from authorities.
  8. Fraud Prevention: Monitoring for suspicious activity to protect you and our business from fraud.

Marketing Opt-Out: You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time by clicking "Unsubscribe" in any email or by emailing privacy@GlossForge.com. Transactional emails (booking confirmations, receipts) will still be sent as they are necessary for service delivery.

Section 04
Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information only with the following categories of recipients and only as necessary:

Service Providers

We engage trusted third-party companies to assist in delivering our services. These include:

  • Payment Processors: Stripe and PayPal (PCI-DSS compliant) for secure payment handling.
  • Email Services: SendGrid for transactional and marketing email delivery.
  • Analytics: Google Analytics (anonymized) for understanding site usage patterns.
  • Booking Software: Our appointment management platform stores booking data securely.
  • Cloud Hosting: Our servers are hosted on AWS with encryption at rest and in transit.

All service providers are contractually bound to protect your data and may not use it for purposes beyond providing services to us.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal processes (such as a subpoena, court order, or government request). We will notify you where permitted by law.

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your data may be transferred to the acquiring entity. We will notify you via email and/or prominent notice on our website prior to any such transfer.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them on a per-site basis.
  • Block third-party cookies from being set.
  • Block all cookies from being set.
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser.

For instructions, visit your browser's help documentation: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge.

Section 06
Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, destruction, or alteration.

  • TLS/SSL Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using 256-bit TLS.
  • Password Hashing: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and are never stored in plain text.
  • Access Controls: Employee access to customer data is role-based and strictly limited to need-to-know.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments of our systems.
  • PCI Compliance: Payment card data is never stored on our servers. All payments are processed by PCI-DSS Level 1 certified providers.

Important: While we implement strong security measures, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately at security@GlossForge.com.

Section 08
Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data. We honor all applicable rights under CCPA, GDPR, and other applicable privacy laws:

Right to Access
Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Right to Rectify
Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

Right to Erasure
Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").

Right to Object
Object to processing of your data for direct marketing.

Right to Portability
Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Right to Restrict
Request that we limit how we process your data.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To submit a privacy rights request, contact us at privacy@GlossForge.com or write to us at our physical address. We will respond within 30 days (or 45 days with notice if your request is complex). We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority if you believe we have violated your privacy rights.

Section 09
Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 16 years. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we learn that we have collected personal data from a child under 16 without verified parental consent, we will delete that information as quickly as possible.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at privacy@GlossForge.com.
