Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?+
You can book via the form on this page, call us at +1 (555) 123-4567, or email info@GlossForge.com. We'll confirm within 1 hour during business hours.
Do I need to drop my car off or do you come to me?+
Both! You can drop off at our studio in Detroit, or book our mobile detailing service. Mobile is available within 30 miles of our studio. Ceramic coating requires studio visit only.
What payment methods do you accept?+
We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), cash, bank transfer, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal. Financing is available for services over $200.
How do I prepare my car for detailing?+
Remove personal belongings and valuables. You don't need to pre-clean your car — that's our job! Just drop it off or let us come to you.
Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?+
Yes. Please give us at least 24 hours notice to reschedule or cancel. Same-day cancellations may incur a $25 administrative fee for large-scale services.
